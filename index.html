What do you do when you leave your MacBook unattended? You worry!

Now with Laplock you can lock your laptop with your power cord.

Make sure the power cord is attached, enable the Laplock Mac app, and if somebody tries to get your laptop while you're away, your laptop will:

  1. Alert everyone around,
  2. Message and call you on your phone

Instructions

  1. Download the Laplock.app file
  2. Open the app to add it to the menu bar
  3. Unplug the power cord from your MacBook and listen to the alarm go off
  4. Worry no more about leaving your MacBook unattended

Telephone Notifications

  1. To receive an SMS messages and phone calls use the "Register Phone" option to add your phone number
  2. To receive a notification through Yo, use the "Register Yo" option

Very Important!

  1. The computer needs an internet connection to trigger the telephone and Yo notifications
  2. Obviously, if your laptop is stolen, we're very sorry, but we're not responsible. We didn't steal it!
