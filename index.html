Instructions

Download the Laplock.app file Open the app to add it to the menu bar Unplug the power cord from your MacBook and listen to the alarm go off Worry no more about leaving your MacBook unattended

Telephone Notifications

To receive an SMS messages and phone calls use the "Register Phone" option to add your phone number To receive a notification through Yo, use the "Register Yo" option

Very Important!

The computer needs an internet connection to trigger the telephone and Yo notifications Obviously, if your laptop is stolen, we're very sorry, but we're not responsible. We didn't steal it!
