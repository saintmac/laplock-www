Instructions

  1. Download the app
  2. Launch the app to add it to the menu bar
  3. Unplug the power cord from your MacBook and listen to the alarm go off
  4. Worry no more about leaving your MacBook unattended

Telephone Notifications

  • To receive a SMS notification us the "Register Phone" option to add your phone number
  • To receive a notification through Yo, use the "Register Yo" option

Disclaimer

  • The computer needs an internet connection to trigger the SMS and Yo notification
  • Your phone needs a cellphone connection to receive the SMS notification
  • Your phone needs an internet connection to receive the Yo notification
  • If your laptop is stolen, we're very sorry, but we're not responsible. We didn't steal it!
