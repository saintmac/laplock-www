How to lock your macbook from your menu bar

Open the Keychain Access app that's included with Mac OS X.

Go to the preferences menu of Keychain Access.

In the General tab, check the "Show keychain status in menu bar" box.

Whenever you want to lock your macbook without shutting it down (because Laplock is running for instance):

  • Click on the Keychain Access lock icon on your mac menu bar
  • Click on "Lock Screen"
